the purpose of this app is to help students in organizing their study hours and the syllabus and have a clear idea of the topics they've studied and the topics which are yet to study. this app contains a timer which helps the students to focus and  track the time they've spent while studying. another feature of  this app is that students can upload cool pictures of themselves studying so that they stay motivated and whenever they feel like not studying, they can check their picture albums and get motivated to study. to-do list is something that every student shoulduse, well this app has it too. students can enter the tasks they want to get done by the end of the day and then check the no. of tasks they've completed which keeps the track of their productivity!
